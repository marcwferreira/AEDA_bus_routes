Detailed Description

This represents a bus stop, it contains:

Parameters
CodeThis is the identifying code of the bus stop
NameThis is the name of the bus stop
ZoneThis is the zone of the city where the bus stop is located at
coordinateThis is the coordinates that the bus stop is located at
lineNeighboursThis is the neighbours (edges) of the bus stop where we can go to by taking a bus
walkNeighboursThis is the neighbours (edges) of the bus stop where we can get to by walking
isVisitedThis is used to during processing to mark if this bus stops was already calculated (visited) in our algorithm or not
